Prosthetics & Orthotics


The cost of prosthetics are a burden to the pockets of amputees. With the price tag being in the tens of thousands, amputees are unable to afford the cost of simplified walking.


Children, adults, and veterans are all amongst those affected by the cost of prosthetics.

Prosthetics should not have a price tag!
